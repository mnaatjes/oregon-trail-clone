# Menus and User Flow (Apple II - 1985)

This document outlines the menu structure and typical user journey in the original game.

## 1. Setup Phase
1.  **Start Menu:**
    - Travel the Trail
    - Learn about the Trail
    - High Scores
    - Management Options
2.  **Character Setup:**
    - Choose Profession (Banker, Carpenter, Farmer).
    - Name the Leader and 4 Companions.
    - Choose Starting Month (March, April, May, June, July).
3.  **Initial Shopping (Matt’s General Store):**
    - Buy Oxen, Food, Clothing, Ammunition, Spare Parts.

## 2. The Main Travel Loop
The screen displays an animation of the wagon and key stats: Date, Weather, Health, Food, Next Landmark distance, and Miles Traveled.

**Options (Accessible via Spacebar):**
- **Continue on Trail:** Advance days.
- **Check Supplies:** View inventory.
- **Look at Map:** View current location on the trail.
- **Change Pace:** Adjust travel speed.
- **Change Rations:** Adjust food consumption.
- **Stop to Rest:** Spend time to heal.
- **Attempt to Trade:** Exchange goods with NPCs.
- **Talk to People:** Gather clues and historical trivia.
- **Hunt:** Enter the hunting mini-game.

## 3. Landmark/River Menus
Triggered automatically when a landmark is reached.
- **At a Landmark:** "Look around," "Talk to people," "Buy supplies" (if at a Fort).
- **At a River:** Choose crossing method (Ford, Caulk, Ferry).

## 4. End Game
- **The Dalles:** Final choice between the Barlow Toll Road or Rafting the Columbia River.
- **Arrival in Willamette Valley:** Final score calculation and entry into the "Wall of Fame."
